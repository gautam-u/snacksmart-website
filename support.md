  <div class="card" style="margin-top: var(--spacing-2xl); text-align: center;">
    <h3>Frequently Asked Questions</h3>

    <div style="text-align: left; margin-top: var(--spacing-xl);">
      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-blue); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">How accurate is the AI food recognition?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">SnackSmart uses on-device Apple Intelligence and advanced ML models to recognize food with high accuracy. For best results, take clear photos in good lighting. You can always manually adjust nutrition values if needed.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-green); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">Is my data secure?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Yes! SnackSmart stores all your nutrition data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal food records or health information.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-purple); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">How do I sync with Apple Health?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">SnackSmart integrates seamlessly with HealthKit. During onboarding or in Settings, grant permissions for the nutrition categories you want to sync. Your data will automatically sync with the Health app.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-orange); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">What's the difference between Free and Premium?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Free tier includes unlimited scans with occasional ads (every 7 scans). Premium removes all ads and unlocks advanced analytics, unlimited scan history, data export (CSV/PDF), and custom dietary filters.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-blue); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">Can I use voice commands to log snacks?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Yes! SnackSmart supports voice logging using Apple's Speech Recognition. Simply say "I ate an apple" and it will be logged automatically. Voice processing happens entirely on-device for privacy.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-green); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">How do I cancel my Premium subscription?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">To cancel your subscription: Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select SnackSmart → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your billing period.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-purple); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">Can I export my nutrition data?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Premium users can export their complete nutrition history in CSV or PDF format from Settings → Export Data. Your data is also accessible through the Health app if you've enabled HealthKit sync.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-orange); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">What iOS version is required?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">SnackSmart requires iOS 17.0 or later to take advantage of the latest Apple Intelligence features and privacy protections.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: var(--spacing-xl);">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-blue); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">Do you support iPad?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Yes! SnackSmart is a universal app that works beautifully on both iPhone and iPad with optimized layouts for each device.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom: 0;">
        <h4 style="color: var(--color-primary-red); margin-bottom: var(--spacing-sm);">How do I restore my Premium purchase?</h4>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">Open SnackSmart → go to Premium/Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription will be restored automatically if you previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
